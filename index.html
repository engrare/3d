Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept? +
We accept .STL and .3MF files directly through our instant quote tool. For other formats (.STEP, .OBJ), please contact us directly.
Do you offer post-processing? +
Yes, standard orders come with support removal. We also offer sanding, smoothing, and painting for custom requests.
What is the molding service? +
For small batch production (10-100 units), we 3D print a master mold and use industrial silicone to cast your parts in resin, offering superior surface finish.
